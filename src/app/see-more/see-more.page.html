Air Quality Information

Learn more about the air quality and its effects on health.

PM2.5

PM2.5 are tiny particles in the air that reduce visibility and cause the air to appear hazy when levels are elevated.

 PM10

PM10 are inhalable particles, with diameters that are generally 10 micrometers and smaller.

 Ozone (O₃)

Ground-level ozone, a harmful air pollutant, affects all of us. It forms when pollutants emitted by cars, power plants, and other sources chemically react in the presence of sunlight.

 Nitrogen Dioxide (NO₂)

Nitrogen Dioxide is a reddish-brown gas with a characteristic sharp, biting odor and is a prominent air pollutant.
